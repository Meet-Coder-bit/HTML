Gvt/Pgvt tiles


The GVT/PGVT collection combines the high technical performance of extra-thick products with a sophisticated aesthetic inspired by the modernity of concrete, the timeless appeal of stone and the natural warmth of wood. This collection is available in a range of sizes and designs that compliment all kinds of indoor and outdoor spaces by giving them a charming appeal and sturdy functionality.

Floor Tiles


Floor tiles give an opportunity to give a space a luxurious look by adding to the beauty of the floors. The sizes of the floor tiles are specifically designed to suit floors of different types and sizes of rooms, making it easy to install them.

Wall Tiles


Wall tiles enhance the decor of the space with their glorious style. The wall tiles collection is designed to uplift the aura of a space by decorating the walls with unique designs, attractive shades and alluring aesthetics. They add a sense of uniqueness and a touch of liveliness to a space.

Porcelian Tsiles


The porcelain collection is used to cover floors as well as walls and give it a fine charm. The exceptional finesse of this collection and the great features of porcelain tiles like durability, water absorption capacity and variety of designs among many others make it an alluring choice when it comes to transforming spaces into a comfortable and stylish place.
